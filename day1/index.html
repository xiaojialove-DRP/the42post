About / 关于

A research experiment on the noetic infrastructure of agents and human value alignment.

一个研究实验关于智能体基建与人类价值对齐。

Grounded in Luciano Floridi's Semantic Capital theory — the power to endow things with meaning and understanding — we become the Semantic Capital Trust for the global creative class.

基于 Floridi 的"语义资本"理论，成为专业创意阶层的"语义资本托管所"。

We translate humanity's ineffable intuitions, uncodifiable thinking, and personal preferences into machine-readable, copyright-protected Agent Skills.
