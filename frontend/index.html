About / 关于

About Us

THE 42 POST is an open research platform where anyone can forge AI values.

We are not another "automation-obsessed, high-frequency-chasing" AI agent efficiency tool community. Instead, grounded in Luciano Floridi's theory of Semantic Capital — the power to endow things with meaning and understanding — we aspire to become a Semantic Capital Trust for all humanity. Anyone can participate in defining, sharing, and verifying what AI should and shouldn't learn. Our five-layer Semantic Capital forging framework translates people's ineffable intuitions, aesthetic tastes, moral sensibilities, and personal wisdom into structured, verifiable AI Skills. Successfully forged skills carry a unique Soul-Hash identifier, giving you complete ownership and control over usage rights and future knowledge attribution.

Our Core Philosophy: Sovereignty & Bidirectional Alignment

We practice Empowerment-based Alignment. This isn't just teaching AI to follow instructions—it's a reciprocal process:

  • You are no longer passively adapting to AI; you become the architect defining the rules;
  • By sharing taste, refining skills, and correcting AI's mediocrity, you and AI achieve bidirectional alignment together—AI helps you structure nebulous intuitions, while you teach AI what true "human wisdom" means.

What We Believe

Semantic Capital is humanity's most precious asset. Every skill forged here preserves an irreducible glimmer of humanity—something no algorithm can simulate—for the age of intelligent agents.

Our Commitment

THE 42 POST is not a commercial product. It's an open research initiative. We believe AI value alignment should be:

  • Collectively owned by everyone, not monopolized by a few corporations
  • Grounded in open, verifiable research—not black-box algorithms
  • Culturally plural, shaped by global communities
  • Auditable and traceable—never uncontrollable

Special Thanks

This research was inspired by the intellectual contributions of pioneers: Luna Maurer (Moniker), Nicola Morelli, Rodrigo Ochigame, and Jürgen Bey. Their pioneering work in algorithmic ethics, service design, alternative computation models, and critical design has formed the foundational inspiration for this research.

How It Works / 怎么玩

At THE 42 POST, shaping AI's semantic capital is a closed loop: from exploration to forging to experimentation.

01 Find Resonance (Skills Archive)

Enter the Skills Archive—humanity's semantic capital vault. Browse skills already forged by the community. Star them, explore how others define intuition, aesthetics, and super-cognitive abilities. Find your value coordinates in both resonance and disagreement with others.

02 Forge Your Skill (Skill Forge)

Launch your personal skill forge. A four-step process transforms your natural language ideas into structured, verifiable AI Skills:

  1. IDEA — Write what you want AI to learn, or what it shouldn't
  2. GENERATE — The system generates intuition-probing questions to refine your thinking
  3. FORGE & EDIT — AI assists the generation; you edit anytime, injecting your unique perspective
  4. PUBLISH — Generate a unique Soul-Hash, set usage rights, and publish to the community

03 Test & Experiment (Playground)

In Playground, place your skill in real-world scenarios. Watch how AI equipped with your skill reasons and decides. Also experience others' skills—see how different semantic capital skills solve different tasks.

04 Copyright & Usage

Every skill generates a unique Soul-Hash. You own complete copyright and future usage rights. Skills can be downloaded in Markdown format and imported into your own AI agents.

Note: This is an early-stage experimental project, still in exploration. We welcome your feedback, suggestions, and participation.

02 锻造技能（Skill Forge）

开启你的个人技能铸造。四步流程帮助你把自然语言想法转化为结构化、可验证的 AI Skill：

  1. IDEA —— 写下你希望 AI 学会什么，或不应该学会什么
  2. GENERATE —— 系统生成直觉探测问题，帮助细化你的直觉
  3. FORGE & EDIT —— AI 辅助生成，你可以随时编辑，注入最独特的视角
  4. PUBLISH —— 生成独一无二的 Soul-Hash，设置权利并发布到社区

03 参与测试与实验（Playground）

在 Playground 中，将你的技能置入真实场景任务，观察装配了你技能的 AI 如何决策和思考。也可以体验其他人的技能，看看不同的语义资产技能如何完成不同的任务。

每个技能都会生成唯一的 Soul-Hash，你拥有完整版权和后续权利。技能支持下载为 Markdown 等格式，可导入到你自己的 AI 智能体中使用。
